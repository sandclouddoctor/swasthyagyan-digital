The mission

Physician-led, evidence-driven, and built for the communities that need it most.

Founded by a practising physician and public health researcher, SwasthyaGyan Digital exists to leverage impact-driven public health programming — improving patient care, strengthening health systems, and designing human-centric digital health solutions.

What we do

Knowledge that moves from study to system

From research design to implementation science and digital health, the work spans the full path from evidence to impact.

Public Health Research

Study design, survey methodology, qualitative questionnaires and literature reviews — built to inform real policy.

Data & Evaluation

Program evaluation and effectiveness analysis using Stata and ATLAS.ti to turn population data into decisions.

Digital Health Solutions

Human-centric digital health and Generative-AI tools designed around how patients and providers actually work.
