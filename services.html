Services

Evidence, translated into impact

SwasthyaGyan Digital pairs clinical practice with research rigour to help organisations design, evaluate and communicate better health programs.

Public Health Research

End-to-end study design — survey methodology, qualitative questionnaires, sampling and literature reviews — built to produce evidence policymakers can act on.

Program Evaluation

Effectiveness and implementation analysis at scale, using Stata and ATLAS.ti to measure what's working — and what to change.

Implementation Science

Bridging the gap between proven interventions and real-world delivery in resource-constrained, frontline health settings.

Digital Health Solutions

Human-centric digital tools and Generative-AI applications designed around the realities of patients, providers and public systems.

Health Systems Strengthening

Quality improvement and assurance — including NQAS certification support — to raise the standard of care at facility and system level.

Health Education & Communication

Behaviour-change communication and accessible health content — including video — that meets communities where they are.

Field tool · live

VAP Family Ledger

A digital data-collection tool built for the AIIMS Bhubaneswar Village Adoption Programme. MBBS students record one ledger per adopted family — the full family questionnaire plus antenatal, postnatal, under-five, chronic-disease, geriatric and adolescent health cards — straight from a phone in the field. Built for the field
